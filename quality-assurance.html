Quality Assurance

At G2Brands our mission statement is to be the best in our field at supplying inspirational and innovative products, which exceed consumer expectation.

Innovation

Products are developed that exceed customer expectation and are innovative and market leading. This includes both product and packaging design.

Product Legislation (GPSR)

All products launched meet UK product legislation (GPSR) and are independently tested by accredited test laboratories.

Diverse Product Range

We aim to provide an exceptional and diverse product range covering Digital, Audio, SDA's, Garden Power, Gaming, Floorcare & Heating/Cooling. Expert product technical support provided.

Ethical Sourcing

All factories are ethically sourced and must follow BSCI / Sedex guidelines and must meet our stringent requirements.

Corporate Social Responsibility

Social responsibility is a core value and is manifested at all levels of our infrastructure. This is something we demand of our suppliers as well.

Product Testing

All of our products go through extensive development programmes with quality and safety paramount. Continual system/product reviews ensure we are supplying products that are first class and exceed customer expectations.
